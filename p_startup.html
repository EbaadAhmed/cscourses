Computer Science Core Subjects

Integer:

These are those numbers which we do not represent in the decimals.

Example:

2 , 5 , 6 ,100,77

Float:

These are those numbers which we represent in the decimals.

Example:

3.5, 6.78, 0.56

Strings:

They are the combination of characters or they are words.

Example:

“hello world” , “hi” , “what is your name?”

Characters:

they are single alphabet .

Example:

‘a’ , ‘b’ , ‘c’

ASCII CODES:

Characters:

A to Z: 65 to 90

a to z: 97 to 122

integers:

0 to 9: 48 to 57
